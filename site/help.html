How to use

In short:
\, L or λ for λ.
Type in a lambda calculus term; get back its reduced normal form!

Beta-Red defines a few common shorthand lambda-calc terms as builtins:

  • 1, 2, and other numbers as their Church numerals
  • plus and multiply, each taking in two numbers
  • pred, the predecessor function
  • true and false as Church booleans
